Module V Focus: Advanced Topics, Ethics, and Project Conclusion

This final module brings the course to a close by exploring forward-looking trends in Big Data analytics, such as the integration of AI, IoT, and Cloud technologies. A critical component of this module is the discussion on data ethics and privacy, equipping you with the framework for responsible data practice. The majority of the time in this module is dedicated to the final phases of your mini-project, including completing your implementation, writing your final report, and preparing your technical presentation. This is where you will synthesize all the knowledge and skills gained throughout the course into a comprehensive, finished product.
